why clarifood?

Have you ever seen someone on the streets and really wanted to know what they were eating? Have you ever seen a picture on Instagram and thought "Wow, I really want to eat that"? Give the picture to clarifood, and you can find restaurants near you that serve the food you want. clarifood is a revolutionary food app that uses a ground-breaking visual recognition API from Clarifai and combines it with the Google Places API to bring you to all those delicious-looking foods.

Why do we need clarifood? Well, if you look at the new generation and the direction we are heading in, the Creative Cloud--clarifood is the future; it has all the tools. Don't me about antiquated tools. I want to tell you about the future.
